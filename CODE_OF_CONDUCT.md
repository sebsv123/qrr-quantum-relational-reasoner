# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the research community
- Be honest about the limits of the work

## Research-Specific Standards

- Do not misrepresent experimental results
- Distinguish clearly between theory and empirical evidence
- Credit prior work appropriately
- Welcome falsification attempts — they make the theory stronger

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or dishonest behavior may be reported to the repository maintainer. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
