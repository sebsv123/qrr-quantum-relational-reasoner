# QRR Logo

The QRR logo mark consists of three overlapping interference rings representing quantum superposition,
with a central teal collapse point (decoherence moment).

## Hosted

The official logo is hosted at:
`https://user-gen-media-assets.s3.amazonaws.com/gpt4o_images/b8c950b9-cc13-4e87-a4fc-96613c5e4e3b.png`

## Colors
- Background: `#0d1117`
- Primary teal: `#01696f`
- Light teal: `#4f98a3`
- White accent: `#ffffff`

## Symbol meaning
- Three rings = K competing hypotheses (superposition)
- Overlapping zones = interference between branches
- Central glow = decoherence / collapse point (χ index)
